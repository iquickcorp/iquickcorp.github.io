Company

A career in the company

Our project is on the active growth stage. Developed a strategy and a clear development plan for the coming years. The company requires active and purposeful specialists who want to realize themselves in the fastest growing and promising segment of the modern economy.

We strive to be the best among the best. Therefore, besides our innovative solutions, we use international experience in solving these problems and attract the best experts in the FinTech industry, the blockchain and cryptocurrency technologies. We offer to become part of the IQuick team and together develop the crypto assets and exchange industry.

Open vacancies

  • 1. We need Promotion specialists in their region (country area or city) to find new partners at this stage of the company development. We are exploring the international market now, so we are looking for partners all around the world. We require candidates to have experience in promoting FinTech products, an understanding of their market for services, creativity, and love for IQuick.
  • 2. Developers in smart contracts (including on the blockchain platform Ethereum) to improve the IQ protocol.
  • 3. Specialists in language localization of the product. If you want to become a partner of the company and consider that the translation of the IQuick service into a local language in your region or country will contribute to its promotion and can make IQuick more accessible to customers, we will gladly consider your offer.
